About Shannon

The builder
agents trust

Shannon Shoare has been building and renovating across Central Victoria for over 15 years, operating from his base in Avenel. Working closely with local real estate agents, Shannon has become the contractor you call when a property needs to be market-ready — fast, done properly, and with no drama.

What sets Shannon apart is breadth. He holds a surveying qualification and operates professional-grade survey equipment — rare in regional Victoria. He also coordinates a trusted network of tradespeople, meaning painting, tiling, cabinetry, flooring, fencing and more can all be managed under one contract.

Whether it's a pre-sale refresh for a Seymour agent or a structural build on an Avenel rural property, Shannon brings the same quality and the same direct communication to every job.

How It Works

Simple from
start to finish

Step 01 Enquire

Call, email or fill in the form. Tell us the property address, what needs doing and your timeline.

Step 02 Site Visit

Shannon visits the property, assesses scope and condition first-hand — and surveys where needed.

Step 03 Quote

A clear, itemised quote with a realistic timeline. No hidden costs, no vague estimates.

Step 04 Schedule

Shannon coordinates all trades and locks in the schedule. One person to call throughout.

Step 05 Build

Work carried out to specification. Shannon is on the tools and on site — not managing from a desk.

Step 06 Handover

Final walkthrough, sign-off and handover. Property is clean, finished and ready — on time.
